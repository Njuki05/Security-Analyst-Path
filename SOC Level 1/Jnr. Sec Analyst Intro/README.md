Introduction to SOC
Platform : Try Hack Me

Objective: Understand what a Security Operations Center is and the roles of people in a SOC environment.

## Key Concepts
Jnr Sec Analyst is also called a SOC Level 1 Analyst.
Roles of a SOC L1 Analyst:
   Monitor and investigate various security alerts.
   Participate in SOC brainstorms and workshops.
   Cooperate with other teams to keep your company safe.
   Constantly learn and discover new attacks and defenses.
